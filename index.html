Chapter 4: The Decision
As night fell once again, the stranger stood at the entrance to the hidden laboratory. The journal had provided clear instructions on how to find it: a sequence of books to be pulled from the library shelves in a specific order.

The stairs descended into darkness. The air grew colder with each step. At the bottom, a heavy iron door barred the way. Inscribed upon it was a warning: "Beyond this threshold lies knowledge not meant for mortal minds. Enter at your own peril."
